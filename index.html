About the Event

HackPulse CTF is a venue-based cybersecurity competition hosted by the Department of Computer Science as part of Interface 2025. This is not your typical eventâ€”this is a live cyber arena where only the fastest and smartest will survive.

Format

The CTF is split into two progressive rounds:

  • Round 1: 10 easy-to-medium challenges in 60 minutes
  • Round 2: 10 hard-to-extreme challenges in another 60 minutes

Challenges include puzzles related to web, forensics, crypto, and more.

Event Schedule

  • 09:00 - 09:30: Team Registration
  • 09:30 - 09:40: Rules Briefing
  • 09:40 - 10:40: Round 1 Begins
  • 10:40 - 11:00: Break & Results
  • 11:00 - 12:00: Round 2 for Top 15 Teams
  • 12:00 - 12:30: Winners & Prizes

Prizes

Winners will receive exclusive tech goodies, certificates, and the right to call themselves the best hackers on campus. All participants receive a certificate of participation.

Rules

No internet access other than the challenge platform. No team may share flags or answers with others. Use of AI, external tools, or search engines is strictly prohibited.
